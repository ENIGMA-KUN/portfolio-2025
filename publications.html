Predicting Emergent Capabilities in Large Language Models

Authors: Shubham Chakraborty, David Williams, Lisa Patel, Robert Chen

This paper introduces a novel framework for quantifying and predicting emergent capabilities in large language models (LLMs) across different parameter scales. Using our proposed E-manifold metrics, we can predict capability emergence with 87% accuracy, significantly outperforming existing methods while reducing computational requirements for evaluation.
